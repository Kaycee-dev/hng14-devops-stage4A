## Teach-Back

OPA is the policy decision point: it answers structured questions using Rego.
The CLI is the policy enforcement point: it gathers facts, asks OPA, explains
the answer, and only then starts or promotes services. Metrics are cumulative
Prometheus samples, so the CLI derives rates and latency windows by comparing
snapshots rather than expecting the app to precompute every policy answer.
